Lead Generation

The goal of this team is to interest doctors and facilities in becoming Docplanner Customers. By using content marketing and automation tools we educate and digitize doctors' offices.

Their main aim is making our customers love us! :) They make our customers engaged in Docplanner and other Dpralians products by building great onboarding and nurturing. Everything is based on analyzing Customers behaviour, data and testing :)

Because together we can do awesome things that make us stand out from the crowd and give us a PR power and competitive edge.
